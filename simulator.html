Train Like Professional Race-Car Drivers Do

A key part of the driving Experience will be the simulator session. The realism, enhanced by the 3D view of the track, will immerse the driver in an experience like no other giving a true sense of depth and speed. While driving, a personal instructor will help the driver to get familiar with the controls of the car; like the no clutch paddle shift and the layout of our circuit.Driving on the simulated race track is the best way to learn it. It is how professional race car drivers train. After the simulator session the driver will be ready for the real excitement to begin. The driver will be able to receive the most out of his Experience from the very first lap!
